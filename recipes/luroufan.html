Lu Rou Fan

Description

Cooked in a rich broth to a melt-in-your-mouth texture, Taiwanese Lu Rou Fan is a heavenly scrumptious way to enjoy pork in an all-in-one rice bowl dish.

Ingredients

  • Pork belly with skin on, cut into strips
  • Shallots (or red onion), thinly sliced, plus oil for frying
  • Shiitake mushrooms, rehydrated and diced Eggs
  • Garlic, minced
  • Rock sugar (or regular sugar)
  • Light & dark soy sauce
  • Shaoxing rice wine (skip for an alcohol-free version)
  • Chinese five-spice powder & white pepper
  • Rice
  • Green vegetable

Steps

  1. 1. Fry sliced shallots in oil until crispy (skip this step if using shop-bought version). Cook hard-boiled eggs over another burner at the same time.
  2. 2.Fry pork belly strips (without oil) until slightly golden then add aromatics, seasonings, fried shallots, shiitake mushroom, eggs and water. Leave to simmer until tender.
  3. 3. While waiting, cook rice and blanch green vegetable of your choice. Assemble the bowl once the pork is done.
